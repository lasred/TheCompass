**How They Handle the Problem Now:**
1. **Send email to client:** "Please send me your tax documents (W-2, 1099, receipts, etc.)"
2. **Wait 3-7 days**
3. **Send follow-up email #1:** "Just checking inâ€”did you get my email about documents?"
4. **Wait another 3-5 days**
5. **Send follow-up email #2:** "We need your documents ASAP to complete your return on time"
6. **Call client if still no response**
7. **Track status in spreadsheet or memory:** "Did Johnson send his W-2? What about his 1099s?"
8. **Client sends documents via email, text, or physical mail** (scattered across channels)
9. **Manually download, rename, and organize documents** into client folders
10. **Repeat for every single client, every engagement**

**Why It's Not Working:**
- **Takes 10-20 hours per month** in follow-ups and organization
- **Documents come via random channels** (email, text, fax, physical mail, Dropbox links)
- **Clients forget what they already sent** ("Did I already send you my W-2?")
- **No visibility into status** ("Which clients have I heard from? Which are still pending?")
- **Password fatigue** if they tried a client portal (clients won't log in)
- **Last-minute chaos** when multiple clients send docs 3 days before deadline
- **Stressful and frustrating** for both firm and clients

**What They've Tried Before:**
1. **Client portals (like Sharefile, SmartVault):**
   - Problem: Clients hate logging in with passwords
   - Problem: Still have to manually remind clients

3. **Email reminders (manual or automated via Outlook rules):**
   - Problem: Clients ignore emails
   - Problem: Still manual tracking
   - Problem: No way to see what's been received vs missing

4. **"I'll just deal with it" mindset:**

## Your Solution

**What You'll Build:**

**Product Name:** DocFlow for CPAs *(or similar)*

**Core Features (MVP):**
1. **Smart Document Request Builder**
   - Pre-built templates for common engagements (1040, 1120S, bookkeeping, etc.)
   - Customizable checklists (W-2, 1099, Schedule C, receipts, etc.)
   - Send requests via email with one click

2. **Multi-Channel Upload (No Password Required)**
   - Client gets email with unique upload link
   - Click link â†’ Upload documents (drag-and-drop)
   - Optional: Reply to email with attachments, or text photos via SMS
   - No login, no password, no portal fatigue

3. **Automated Reminders**
   - Email + SMS reminders on custom schedule (e.g., every 3 days)
   - "You've sent 3 of 7 documents. Still need: W-2, 1099-INT, Charitable receipts"
   - Stops reminding when checklist is complete

4. **Progress Dashboard for Firm**
   - See all clients at a glance: Green (complete), Yellow (partial), Red (not started)
   - Sort by deadline urgency
   - One-click to send manual follow-up if needed
   - Documents auto-organized into client folders

5. **Client-Friendly Experience**
   - Simple checklist: "You've sent 3 of 7 documents"
   - Confirmation when complete: "All done! We'll be in touch when your return is ready."
   - Works on mobile (clients can upload from phone)
