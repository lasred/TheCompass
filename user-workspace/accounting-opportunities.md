### The Business:

- **Firm size:** Solo firms ($62K revenue) to small firms ($1M+ revenue)

- **Client volume:** 20+ clients minimum

   - Tracking which docs are received vs missing (spreadsheets or memory)
   - Average 10-20 hours/month wasted on follow-ups

2. **Client Confusion**
   - Vague requests: "Send me your tax docs"
   - Clients forget what they already sent
   - Password fatigue prevents portal login
   - Documents sent via random channels (email, text, physical mail, fax)

3. **Deadline Pressure**
   - Tax deadlines non-negotiable (April 15, Oct 15, etc.)
   - Missing client docs = can't complete work = file extension = unhappy client
   - Late filings = penalties for client = firm's fault in client's eyes

4. **Lost Revenue**
   - Can't bill until work is complete
   - Delayed cash flow while waiting for docs
   - Some firms don't bill for time spent chasing (unbilled hours)

### Why They'll Pay ($10K-$20K value annually):

**Time Savings:**
- Firm with 50 clients, 3 follow-ups per client, 5 min per follow-up = 12.5 hours/month
- At $200/hour billing rate = **$2,500/month** = **$30,000/year** in recoverable time

**Deadline Protection:**
- Preventing one missed deadline (file extension, penalty) saves $500-$2,000
- Better client experience = retention = $5K-$50K in lifetime value per client

**Cash Flow Improvement:**
- Faster document receipt = faster work completion = faster billing = cash flow boost

- **Firm size:** Solo to 20-person firms
- **Geography:** Any
- **Billing model:** Hourly or value-based with time tracking

### Pain Points:

   - Forget to start timer, reconstruct time at end of week (inaccurate)

2. **Write-Offs**
   - Firm doesn't bill for all hours worked
   - "I can't bill the client for 10 hours when I said it would take 6"
   - Average firm writes off 10-15% of time = lost revenue

3. **Billing Delays**
   - Manual invoice creation from time sheets
   - Takes 2-5 days to create invoices after month-end
   - Delayed billing = delayed cash flow

4. **Client Surprise**
   - Bill client $5K when they expected $3K
   - Leads to complaints, negotiation, bad reviews

### Why They'll Pay ($20K-$40K value annually):

**Capture Unbilled Hours:**
- Firm with $500K revenue, 15% write-off rate = **$75K in unbilled time**
- Recapture even 50% of that = **$37,500/year**

**Faster Billing = Better Cash Flow:**
- Reduce invoice creation from 5 days to 1 day
- Get paid 4 days faster = cash flow improvement

**Client Satisfaction:**
- Transparent time tracking = no surprise bills = happier clients = retention
