**Objective:** Research three niches sequentially, identify at least one painful problem worth $20K+ in each, then compare all three to determine which offers the best opportunities

**Timeline:** 1 week

**No outreach yet.** This is pure research.

## What You're Doing This Week

1. Learn The Boring Problems Framework
2. Pick 3 industries to explore
3. For EACH industry (sequentially):

## Why This Matters

You can't build a business without a customer.

You can't close a customer without a problem worth solving.

Mission 1 teaches you to see opportunities everywhere.

## The 4 Criteria

A problem is worth solving if it meets ALL 4:

1. **Manual Process Hell** - paper, spreadsheets, manual work
2. **High-Frequency Repetition** - 10+ times per month
3. **Compliance or Revenue-Critical** - must do it or can't operate/bill
4. **Terrible Existing Software** - doesn't exist, too expensive, or outdated

**When all 4 are met: This is gold.**
